# Glossary

- STT: Securities Transaction Tax applied on stock trades.
- STCG: Short Term Capital Gains tax.
- LTCG: Long Term Capital Gains tax.
- SIP: Systematic Investment Plan.
- XIRR: Return calculation for irregular investments.
- NAV: Net Asset Value.
- AUM: Assets Under Management.
- CAGR: Annual growth rate.
- Equity: Ownership in a company.
- Bond: Fixed income investment.
- Liquidity: Ease of selling asset.
- Volatility: Price fluctuation.
- Yield: Income return.
- Expense Ratio: Fund fee.
- NFO: New Fund Offer.
